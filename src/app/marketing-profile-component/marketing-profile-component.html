Frequently Asked Questions

Are you SEBI registered?

No. Lynqa Strategic Finance does not provide investment advice or distribute financial products. We do not fall under SEBI’s registration mandate.

What exactly do Lynqa offer?

We assist companies with capital strategy, deal negotiation, documentation, and structuring - specifically around debt instruments like commercial papers and NCDs.

What is the advantage of raising funds with Commercial Papers (CP)?

Commercial Papers (CPs) are short-term debt instruments that offer companies a faster, cheaper and cleaner way to access working capital without the baggage of long-term loans or bank negotiations. They’re typically issued at a lower interest rate than other debt options and don’t require collateral, making them ideal for creditworthy corporates looking to bridge liquidity gaps or optimize cash flow.

What is the advantage of raising funds with Non-Convertible Debentures (NCDs)?

NCDs are long-term instruments that allow companies to lock in structured debt at fixed tenures and attractive yields without giving up equity or control. They're especially powerful for firms aiming to build predictable liability profiles.

Do you help investors?

We do not manage investor capital or offer investment advice. Our engagement is with companies seeking to raise funds at optimal terms.

Can you draft term sheets or support documentation?

Yes, we assist in drafting term sheets, coordinating documentation, and facilitating clean execution of structured debt deals.
